Password World

    About Password World

    This is a modern password generation tool designed to create strong and secure passwords. It allows you to customize the length and character types of the password to suit your needs.

    This app does not store any passwords. The passwords are generated on the client-side and are not transmitted or stored anywhere. This ensures that your passwords remain private and reduces the risk of unauthorized access. However, it is important to follow good password management practices to enhance the security of your passwords.

    You can check out the source code on GitHub.
